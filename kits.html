## Read Me

This is a placeholder for collecting kits for the Pocket Operator K.O 33.

The pocket operator allows you to export and import the sounds and patterns that you make while creating a jam. Others can import your kit and start jamming right away too.

Add New Kit

To upload a new kit, you'll need to have the following files.

  1. Kit Export: export the entire kit as a .flac. Should sound like white noise.
  2. Beat Export: loop through all the patterns as a .mp3
  3. Video Jam: get your phone out and take a short video of you jamming to the beat you made.
